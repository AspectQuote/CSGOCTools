Scripts

CSGOClicker scripts are used to assist in automation menial tasks (Such as unboxing and selling 1,000,000,000 Mil-Spec skins) so that you can gamble your heart out. In order to use scripts, you will need to install Tampermonkey, and have some decent knowledge on how to use it. Generally, CSGOClicker scripts come in two types: Mission and Unboxing. Mission scripts will automate missions for you, allowing you to get a much cash as you need, provided you have enough time. Unboxing scripts will assist in unboxing and selling your items, and will keep running until it runs out of money or your inventory is full of decent skins.

In order to install each of these scripts, you will need to have Tampermonkey installed on your browser. To get script updates, you will have to come back to the greasyfork page and click 'reinstall'. These scripts were created by and uploaded to greasyfork by XingyCoderXYZ
